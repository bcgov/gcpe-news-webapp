How to get vaccinated for COVID-19

There are three steps to get vaccinated. Register, book an appointment and get the vaccine. 

Last updated: March 29, 2021 

There are three steps to get vaccinated

Step 1: Register

When you register you get a confirmation number. You use that number to later book your vaccination appointment.

Registering also helps health authorities request the right amount of vaccine doses for each community.

Step 2: Book an appointment

When you are contacted to book a vaccine appointment, you will select a location, date and time.

You can book your appointment online or by phone. 

Step 3: Get the vaccine

Visit the immunization clinic to get your vaccine dose.

People who get their first vaccine dose will be notified by email, text or phone call when they are eligible to book an appointment for their second dose.

What you need to register

To register, you'll be asked for:

  • First and last name
  • Date of birth 
  • Postal code
  • Personal Health Number

You can find your Personal Health Number on the back of your B.C. driver's licence or BC Services Card. If you do not have a Personal Health Number, you can still get the vaccine.

We'll also ask you for contact information:

  • An email address that gets checked regularly
    or
  • A phone number that can receive text messages

This is how we'll contact you to book a vaccination appointment reservation.

We will never ask you for your SIN, driver's licence number or banking and credit card details.

There are two options to register

Register online

You can register for yourself or for someone else, like a parent or grandparent. It's safe and your records will not be made public.

It's open 24 hours a day.

Register online

Register by phone

We understand you may want to register by phone and that's okay. If call volumes are high, this may take longer. 

Call: 1-888-268-4319 
International: 1-604-412-0957
Telephone for the Deaf: Dial 711 
Translation services: 1-888-263-4319

After you register 

Get your confirmation number

When you submit your information, you receive a confirmation number. Do not lose the number.

This number makes it easier when you book your vaccination appointment.

Wait to be contacted

We’ll contact you when you are eligible to book a vaccine appointment, based on B.C.'s COVID-19 Immunization Plan phases. 

We want everyone in B.C. to register. Spread the word and help your friends and family complete their registration.
